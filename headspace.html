Headspace
The idea for Headspace was born in summer of 2020 where everything stood still and i suddenly had a huge gap to fill. During that time, i started going on walks around the city and took photos of things that caught my eye. Iâ€™m always very hesitant to take photos on the street but every evening after developing i had at least one picture that i liked. This kept me going and made these walks my daily routine. After about 6 months and almost a dozen rolls of black and white film, i felt the project was mature enough to have its own publication. In the beginning of 2021, i released the zine in a limited edition of 25 copies. Having control over the whole creation process including photography, development and finally layouting and designing the printed version was a really fun process and sparked my love for print publications.
